Nonprofit 990 Data Processor

Upload a CSV file with two columns, 'ein' and 'year'. Each line is counted as a single entry, a single filing to be retrieved and processed.

For example, a single, proper entry would have '123456789' under the ein column and '2019' under the year column. The tool will download, process, and provide a final data extract for you.
